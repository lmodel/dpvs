## Comments

* Environment here is a broad term used to refer to the immediate
surroundings and parts of it that form the context of where the
technology operates, for example a room or in open air where specific
machines are set up. This helps describe the expectations and set up of
systems and, to specify and compare them against ideal conditions or
environments
