## Comments

* PotentialImpact is a suggestion that the concept can be a 'risk' within
an use-case - this suggestion is not exclusive and the concept may also
be instances of other potential concepts to indicate the multiple
possible roles a concept can take. This suggestion can be ignored if it
is not applicable to the use-case
